A blast that shookhouses in the north and east part of Denton occurred about midnight Saturday, waking many citizens in those parts of town. Officers investigating found a large hole in the ground east of the railroad tracks, and belive that thte explosion was part of the dynamite stolen serval weeks ago from the tibbetts construction company. No clue has been found as to who set off the explosive.
