Rosetta Crawford, wife of Bert Crawford, a well known young negro living on the corner of Withers street and Bell avenue, died Tuesday night with a complication of diseases. She was about thirty years old and had been in critical condition for the past several weeks. Bert Crawford, her husband, is an employe of the L.T Millican Lumber Co. and is well liked by the white-citizens.
The Crawford Funeral home, also known as Citizens Undertaking Funeral Parlor, was owned by Bert Crawford's son, Ford.Funeral Procession for Rosetta Crawfrord,1910, Collection
