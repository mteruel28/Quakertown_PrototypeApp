Accessibility Features


These features have been incorporated into our website to increase the accessibility of the website and hopefully increase the number of people that are able to experience Quakertown.


  • Color blind considerations
  • Detailed descriptions of the photos and historical importance for those that are visually impaired
  • Optional videos for those that would like to download the content but that do not start automatically
  • Large buttons that are clear and easy to read
  • When you zoom into the map the text also gets larger (unlike google maps where the text on the roads stays small when you zoom in)
  • An option to explore the town without having to move around the area to the geolocations, for those with limited mobility, or health concerns
  • Audio and Subtitle Feature for Visually Impaired
  • Offline Feature for Users with not access to Wifi
